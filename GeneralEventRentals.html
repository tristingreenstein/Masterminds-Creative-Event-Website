General Event Rentals

You're planning a party for 25 people, but only have enough chairs for 10; Your event location does not have the linen color you always dreamed of; The weather changed and suddenly your outdoor event will be challenged with cold weather; Masterminds Creative Events will handle all your rental needs.

Avoid the expense and hassle of purchasing “one time” rental items and then finding a spot to store them. Our rental inventory is expansive and you will find everything you need to plan the perfect event within your budget.

For over 30 years we have been providing our customers with quality rental products that are delivered when promised, clean and ready to go the day of your event and are picked up in a timely manner.    When you’re ready, we’re ready!
