Virtual Reality Is All the Craze
Get ready to step into the future and beyond

  • Posted By : Allen Greenstein - President MCE
  • |
  • Time : 5.32 pm

With Virtual Reality rentals from Masterminds Creative Events, you can enter into worlds you have only dreamed about! With Virtual Reality almost anything is possible. Masterminds makes creating a whole new world at your next event possible!

Masterminds will Bring the Experience to You

Everyone is talking about Virtual Reality these days. The newest craze that will keep your guests entertained for hours and bring a smile to everyone’s faces! VR has become popular at all kinds of events including corporate gatherings, Bar/Bat Mitzvahs, family reunions, and any other type of party that you can think of. We can bring a singular limited safe contact virtual reality rental station to your event at your home, office or unique facility. We are here to provide information on this exciting, growing technology that is tons of fun!

 Virtual Reality as “a technology by which computer-aided stimuli create the immersive illusion of being somewhere else.” Someone who is operating a computer-generated VR program becomes part of a virtual world and becomes immersed in its environment.

What World Do You Want to be a Part of?

At Masterminds, we have plenty of Virtual Reality games to choose from, and trust us, you can’t go wrong with any of them!

Our most popular game by far is Beat Saber! Beat Saber is a unique VR rhythm game where your goal is to slash the beats (represented by small cubes) as they are coming at you with your virtual light saber. This is a great option to get you and your guests familiar with virtual reality and it is loads of fun too!

Virtual Reality uses VR goggles to show you to how you 360-degree moves! We have different kind of movies which will show you different experiences such launch into outer space with our Galaga style Space Pirate Trainer, jump to the wild west with Guns n’ Stories, become James Bond meets the Matrix with first person shooter Super Hot, diffuse a bomb with your friends with Keep Taking and Nobody Explodes, crazy roller coasters, fighting warrior games or a favorite Tilt Brush which places you as an artist in multiple scenic destinations designing in a 360 degree platform!

VR doesn’t only bring you to worlds you’ve never encountered; it also offers you the opportunity to experience exhilarating activities! You don’t have to go to the amusement park to ride a roller coaster! You can even travel to any beach you can imagine and surf the big waves! All of this, and more, is possible at your next party or event with our help.

There are many experiences just waiting to be explored, all you have to do is decide where you want to go or what you want to do, and Masterminds Creative Events will make it possible at your next event! See for yourself why VR is what everyone is talking about!
