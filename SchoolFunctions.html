School Functions

School events are important for community spirit. Common events at school are important for students both socially and academically, especially if they are well organized.

Getting prepared for large school events can be challenging and overwhelming if you do not have guidance. Masterminds Creative Events can be that guide having successfully been a part of over 5000 school events. We will take the time to listen, provide leadership and interact with your schools ASB Leadership Team, PTA Groups,  Special Events Directors and Volunteers to bring their visions to life!

Tell us more about your event

Each school has a unique budget for events and our goal is to work within your guidelines while thinking outside of the box for bigger and better events each year. We want you to have the consistency of a professional organization in the background while reflecting the exciting and fun desires of the students and parents at the events.

SCHOOL DANCES • HOMECOMING • SENIOR PROM • GRADUATION CEREMONIES • GRAD NIGHT • PARENT FUNDRAISERS - CASINO NIGHTS | GOLF TOURNAMENTS | DINE OUT NIGHTS • TEAM BANQUETS • SENIOR BREAKFAST • PEP RALLYS • CLUB PARTIES • PTA EVENTS • END OF YEAR CELEBRATIONS
